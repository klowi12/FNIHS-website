About Us

Welcome to FNIHS

Famy National Integrated High School is a public high school located in the municipality of Famy, Laguna in the Philippines. The school is committed to providing quality education to its students and has a strong focus on academic excellence, cultural preservation, and social responsibility. Famy National Integrated High School offers various academic tracks, including Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM), and Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), among others. The school is also proud of its strong sports and cultural programs, which promote a well-rounded education and foster the holistic development of its students.
